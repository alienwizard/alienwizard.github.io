Neon Dark is a game that im currently developing with a couple of friends. Im doing the scripting and implementation into Unreal engine 4 and my two friends are doing the 3D modelling and texturing.
The game is set in a space station where you are awoken from your cryogenic sleep to fix a ship malfunction. We wanted to give tha game a special look and fell. We decided to make the game with one mental image on our head: Darth Vader in a strip club. The result of this is alot of black and aluminium, and alot of neon.
