Robo-priest is a First person adventure game that i made in Unreal development kit. The player takes the role of a robot that has been activated to preform a sacred ritual.
The civilization that made you is long gone. In their dying moments they constructed massive temples in honor of the gods.
They wanted to make sure that their gods would be worshiped forever, so they constructed robot priest to be activated to preform the most sacred of rituals.
